About me

Yukiko Sato is an assistant professor at the Graduate School of Media and Governance and German lecturer at Keio University in Japan. She lecturers Media&Communication at Showa Women's University (SWU) in Tokyo. She is also an associate researcher at Cygames Research. She received a B.A. (Policy Management) from Keio University in 2014, acquired double degrees from Keio University and Martin-Luther University Halle-Wittenberg in Germany in 2016 for her M.A. and earned a Ph.D. (Media and Governance) from Keio University in 2019. Currently, her major research project involves analyzing and comparing media content and language, studying the mechanics and cultural meaning of video games.

She was born in Japan, brought up in San Diego, CA., and studied abroad for a year in Germany. She is able to communicate in Japanese, English, and German.
